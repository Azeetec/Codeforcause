About us

Code for cause will be a platform where developers can support non-profit organizations with their skills instead of money. It will connect the software developers and the small organizations who want help in Information Systems. The client of this project is the non-profit organizations, that don’t have the budget to hire a company or individual for required IT Work. The clients also include developers who are looking to support any organization/cause with their time and skill and the naïve developers that are new in the field of software development and are looking to make live projects to make their portfolio. The platform will end the search of the people who want to get the development jobs and will also end the search of the organizations who want to find developers for software development in different technologies.
